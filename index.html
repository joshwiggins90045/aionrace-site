Live race intelligence

Know what's happening before it happens.

AION watches every lap in real time and surfaces insights that used to require an experienced eye on the finish line.

AION watches whether each racer's laps are getting faster or slower — flagging who's in a groove and who's fading. No manual lap splits required.

Live series standings

Championship leaderboard updates in real time as each race is completed. No manual tallying after the fact.

How to get started

Why AION

Time isn't just a number.
It's a story.

AION comes from the ancient Greek αἰών — not clock time (chronos), but the continuous, unbroken flow of time. The kind that connects every lap you've ever ridden into a single arc. The kind that makes a race more than a finishing position.

Chronos vs AION

Chronos is the clock — minutes and seconds. AION is the journey — every race, every rival, every improvement over a season. We track both, but we care about the second one.

AI that understands context

Our AI doesn't just report results — it reads the race. Who pushed whom. Who faded. Who found a gear in the final lap. Context that turns a timestamp into a story worth sharing.

Every racer, every race

AION remembers. A junior racer's first podium. A masters rider's season-best lap. The moment someone finally beat their rival. The full arc — not just today's snapshot.

"We named it AION because great races deserve more than a spreadsheet. They deserve a story."
